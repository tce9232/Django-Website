Mission Statement

The Data Collective is a collection of data-driven articles written by professionals in data science or related fields. People in these fields tend to run incredibly insightful analyses and are highly motivated to shed light on complex issues, but most of this time and energy usually ends up going towards improving the bottom line of a tech giant or consulting firm. This blog aims to become an outlet for data scientists and others to analyze and post about topics in which they're independently passionate.
